HAPL Teen Advisory Board Reaches 500 Summer Volunteer Hours!

Shortly before the school year began, the Hudson Area Public Library Teen Advisory Board; known as TAB reached 500 volunteer hours throughout the summer.

What does TAB do?

They are a group dedicated to helping the library. They have meetings twice a month to discuss changes that can be made at the library. They also help with various library events, including but not limited to; the TAB carnival, reptile parties, various craft-related classes, and decorating the library for holidays.

What does this mean for TAB?

Due to a their effort of a combined 500 hours of hard work to help the library, they got to have an after hours party on Friday, August 26th to celebrate their past and continued effort to help the community.

I wish every community had a group like this

-Emilia Reynolds, director of TAB
